## Document Structure

Guides are organized by **Category** and **Document ID** for easy navigation. Each language folder follows the same naming convention: `{lang}_{category}_{id}.md`.

### General (01–03)

Basic account and app settings.

- **01** Easy One-touch Login & Sign-up
- **02** Privacy & Data Security
- **03** Exploring the 'More' Tab

### Calendar (10–13, 19)

Viewing and managing your schedule.

- **10** Overview: Schedule at a Glance
- **11** Switching Schedule View Modes
- **12** Understanding Shift Summary Cards
- **13** Calendar Sync & Management
- **19** Calendar Notification Settings

### Shift Management (20–23, 29)

Personal duty and shift tools.

- **20** Smart Personal Shift Management
- **21** Customizing Shift Types
- **22** How to Use the Shift Table
- **23** Managing Leave & Half-day Requests
- **29** Shift Notification Settings

### Workplace (30–36)

Team collaboration and admin tools.

- **30** Introduction to Our Workplace
- **31** Workplace Shift Type Management
- **32** Create, Join, Leave, or Delete a Workplace
- **33** Admin Features & Usage Guide
- **34** Viewing the Full Team Shift Table
- **35** Workplace Bulletin Board
- **36** Exporting Shift Table as an Image

### Social & Collaboration (40–421)

Connecting with friends and groups.

- **40** Creating & Managing Groups
- **41** Adding and Removing Friends
- **420** Group Shift Table Access
- **421** Group Bulletin Board Usage
