## **How are Shift Types Managed?**

Items like DAY, NIGHT, and OFF displayed on your work **schedule** are called **Shift Types**.
CareWokx allows **Shift Types** to be managed differently for individuals and teams.

---

## **🧩 Default Shift Types Provided**

When you first install the app, the following **Shift Types** are provided by default.
This configuration is used commonly for both **individuals** and **teams**.

- **Standard Shifts**: DAY, EVE, NIGHT, MID-DAY, SPLIT, 24H
- **Custom Shifts**: C1, C2, C3 (You can freely change these names)
- **Time-Off Types**: OFF, R (Annual Leave), ½ (Half-Day Leave)

---

## **⚙️ Who Manages Shift Types?**

**Shift Types** are managed in the two ways described below:

### **1\. My Shift Types (Set by Individual)**

- A basic set is provided when you install the app.

- You can modify names, add/delete items, and set notifications.

- The **Notification feature** plays music at a set time. It only works when your device is set to Ringtone Mode.

- The **Shift Types** you modify **remain unchanged, even if you join or leave a different team.**

### **2\. team Shift Types (Set by team)**

- When you join a team, the **Shift Types** defined by that team are automatically applied.

- team **Shift Types** can **only be modified by the administrator**. (**Notifications** can be set individually.)

---

## **Q. What happens to Shift Types when a team is deleted?**

If you tap **[My team Info]** at the top of the **[team]** tab, a list of teams you have belonged to is shown.
If you delete an old team (e.g., 'team 1') from this list:

- **The Shift Types used in that team are automatically converted to your personal Shift Types**, and
- **Your work schedule will no longer be visible to the members remaining in that team.**

---

## **Q. How are the Shift Start Date and Schedule Viewing Range set?**

When you join a team, your **Join Date is automatically set as the Shift Start Date.**

- If an older work **schedule** is needed, **the administrator must adjust the Shift Start Date.**

---

## **📌 In Summary,**

- Your personal **Shift Types** and the team **Shift Types** are **saved separately.**

- Even if you leave or delete a team, **the entered work schedule does not disappear.**

- The **Shift Start Date** is automatically set, but **can be adjusted by the administrator.**
