## **Shift Summary Card**

By **swiping up or tapping a specific date** on the monthly **calendar** screen, you can view that day’s **Shift Summary Card** and **calendar list** at a glance.

Since you can see your **shift calendar** and personal **calendar** together, **it’s easy to make appointments or plan your schedule based on your shift hours.** For example, you can check your shift end time and schedule personal **calendar** items afterward, or adjust your schedule using half-day leave.

**The Shift Summary Card displays the following information:**

- **Shift Type** (e.g., D, N, OFF, Annual Leave, etc.)

- **Shift Hours**

- **Notification Settings** (Status)

- **Shift Memo Content**

### **🔍 Are OFF days not displayed on the calendar?**

Days marked as OFF (non-working days) do not have a separate indication on the **calendar**.

This can make it confusing to tell if the day is **an OFF day or if the shift just hasn't been entered yet.** In this case, tap the date. If you previously entered a **shift calendar** item, it will be displayed on the screen for confirmation.
