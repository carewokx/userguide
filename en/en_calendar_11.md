## **Calendar View Modes**

The [Calendar] tab offers a total of 3 view modes.
Feel free to switch between them depending on the situation to manage your calendar efficiently.

---

### **① Monthly View**

You can view the entire month's calendar at a glance.

📌 Provided as the default screen.
📌 To add a personal calendar item, tap the **'Add Calendar Item'** button on the bottom right.

---

### **② Monthly/Daily Combined View**

You can view the selected day's calendar and the shift summary card together.

📌 Switch to this mode by **tapping a date** in the [Monthly View] or **swiping up**.
📌 **Adding/editing/deleting** calendar items and **adding stickers** are all possible.

---

### **③ Daily View**

Only the selected day's calendar is displayed in a large view.

📌 Switch to this mode by **swiping up** from the [Monthly/Daily Combined View] state.
📌 You can return to the Monthly/Daily Combined View by **swiping down** on the top of the screen.
📌 Calendar management and sticker addition are also possible in this mode.

---

## **🔄 View Mode Switching Tips**

- **Change Mode**

  - **Swipe up or down** on the monthly area
  - **Drag the handle** at the top of the daily view

- **Date Navigation**

  - **Monthly Navigation**: Swipe left or right on the monthly area
  - **Daily Navigation**: Swipe left or right on the daily area

---

## **Stickers**

Easily mark your personal routines or special days with stickers!

- **How to Add**
  Tap the **'+' button** on the top right of the [Daily View] screen to select the desired sticker.
  You can place up to **2 stickers** per date.

- **How to Remove**
  Simply delete a sticker by tapping the sticker you have placed and then pressing 'Remove Sticker'.
