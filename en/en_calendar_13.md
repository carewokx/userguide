## **📅 Calendar & Calendar Management**

---

## **Calendar Management**

In the [more > Calendar Setting] menu, you can view the list of linked calendars and select which calendars to display. Try importing the calendars you originally used for convenient management.

- The following calendars can be linked:

- CareWokx internal **calendar**

- Google **Calendar**

- Apple **Calendar** (iPhone)

- Samsung **Calendar** (Samsung Phones)

- Other system-registered **calendars**

---

## **Calendar Item Management**

### **Add Calendar Item**

  You can add a new **calendar** item by tapping the **'Add Calendar Item'** button located at the bottom.

  - **If you 'Add Calendar Item' when a date is already selected, the calendar item is automatically assigned to that date.**

  - You can set the **calendar item name**, **date**, **start time**, and **end time**.

  - By turning on the **'All Day'** toggle, you can set only the date without a specific time.

  - The **calendar** where the item is saved is set to the **CareWokx Calendar** by default, but you can choose another linked **calendar** for saving.

  - **Notifications** are set to **1 hour before** by default. You can turn them off or adjust them to **5 minutes before**, **10 minutes before**, etc., as needed.

  - You can also write a **memo** together with the item.

### **Edit Calendar Item**

  To edit a **calendar** item you have already added, select the relevant date and tap the item to modify it.

  - You can change the item's **name**, **time**, **notification settings**, and **memo**.

  - Be sure to press **Save** when you are finished with your modifications.

  - However, **the calendar where the item was first saved cannot be changed.**

### **Delete Calendar Item**

  To delete a **calendar** item, tap the item and then press the **'Delete'** button at the bottom of the screen.
