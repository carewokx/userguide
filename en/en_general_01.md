## **Easy One-Touch Login 🔑**

Keep your personal information safe and log in fast!
Easily get started with your Google, Facebook, or Apple (iPhone only) account.

---

**🔐 How to Sign Up / Log In**
You can easily sign up and log in using your Google, Facebook, or Apple account.
The app connects directly with the social account that is permitted on your device, without complicated verification steps.

- **Information Used**

  - Profile Photo: The photo set on your login account is used automatically.

  - Email: The linked email is retrieved and used. (*Note: If the same email is already registered, a message will appear indicating that it is already in use.*)

  - Apple Login allows you to use a virtual email address instead of your actual email.

  - Nickname: The nickname from your social account is retrieved and used.
     → You can modify it anytime in **[More > My Info]**.

  ***

- **Information Created**
  Upon completion of sign-up, your own unique identification number is created.
- You can check it in **[More > My Info]**.
- This identification number is used when **joining a workplace/group** or **adding friends**.

---

**⚠️ Caution When Changing Devices**
If you plan to switch from an iPhone to another device, please link an additional Google or Facebook account beforehand.
Apple Login will not appear on non-iPhone devices.
