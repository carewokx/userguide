## **🤝 Groups for Your Loved Ones 👥**

Share your **shift** schedule with friends and family and coordinate your **calendar** more conveniently!

## **Groups**

**Check the shift schedules of members in the groups you belong to, and communicate via the bulletin board.**

### **Create a Group**

  - You can create a group by simply entering the group name.

### **Join a Group**

  - You can belong to up to 20 groups.

  - You can join immediately by entering the group's identification code.

### **Invite to a Group**

  - **Invite from Friend List**: You can invite friends from your CareWokx friend list. The invitation link is valid for 24 hours.

  - **Invite with Identification Code**: Even if they are not yet your friends, you can directly enter the invitee's identification code to invite them. This invitation is also valid for 24 hours.

  - **Send Invitation Link**: You can invite friends by sharing the generated invitation link through other messenger apps, etc.

### **Group Operation**

  - Unlike a workplace, there is no separate administrator within a group.

  - The group's maintenance period is **60 days**.

  - In [Group Settings], you can reset the maintenance period to 60 days at any time when the remaining period is 59 days or less.

  - You can also delete the group or leave it in [Group Settings].

### **Group Bulletin Board**

  - Similar to the workplace bulletin board, you can register, edit, and delete posts.

  - Share important news with your group members.

  ***

## **Friends**

'Add Friends' and invite them to groups or workplaces anytime.

### **Add Friend**

  - You can add a friend by entering the identification code of the person you want to add.

  - Once the other person accepts, they will be added to your friend list.

  - You can send an invitation link; if the recipient accesses the link, they are automatically added to your friend list.

### **Delete Friend**

  - The delete button appears when you swipe left on the friend's name in the friend list.
