## **Team Creation/Joining/Leaving/Deletion**

### **🔹 team Creation and Joining**

You can easily create and join a team. However, you can only be affiliated with one team at a time.

- **team Creation**

  - A team is created by simply entering the team name and your name.

  - You can invite colleagues right after creation to use it together.

- **team Joining**

  - You can join instantly by entering the **identification code**, **password**, and the name you wish to use.

  - You can also join by accepting an invitation from an existing member.

  ***

### **🔹 team Member Invitation**

You can join by invitation even if you don't know the identification code or password.

**👥 Invite from Friend List (Valid for 24 hours)**

- Only friends who can be invited are displayed in the list.
  **🔑 Invite with Personal Identification Code (Valid for 24 hours)**
- Each user has a unique 8-digit identification code.
- Your personal identification code can be checked under your name in the **[More]** tab.
  **🔗 Share Invitation Link**
- You can invite by sharing the link via messenger or social media.

❓ **If "User cannot be invited" is displayed**

- User does not exist (incorrect identification code)
- User has deactivated the app
- User is already affiliated with another team
- User has already received an invitation

  ***

### **🔹 How to Leave a team**

**General Member**

- **Immediate Leave**: **[team > team Info > Settings > Leave team]**

- **Scheduled Leave**: Set the **shift** end date in **[team > team Info > Settings > Change My Info]** (automatic leave at midnight of that date)

**Administrator**

- If you have administrator privileges, you cannot leave directly.

- You can only leave after another administrator removes your administrator privileges.

- If the administrator is the last remaining member, the only way to leave is by deleting the team.

  ***

### **🗑️ team Deletion and Password Reset**

teams that are no longer in use can be deleted.

- The password set during creation is required for deletion. Use **Reset** if you don't know the password.

⚠️ When a team is deleted, **all members are automatically removed**. Please decide carefully.

---

### **📌 Frequently Asked Questions**

**Q. Where can I check the teams I previously worked at?**

- In **[team > team Info]**, you can check the list of current and past teams, their identification codes, and activity periods.

**Q. What happens if I delete a team from the past team list?**

- Your work record remaining in that team's schedule disappears, and the **Shift Type** for that period in your personal work schedule changes to the type you have set personally.
