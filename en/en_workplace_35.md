## **Team Bulletin Board**

A **dedicated bulletin board only viewable by Team members** is provided in **[Team > Bulletin Board]**.

Due to the nature of shift work, it's often difficult to announce notices to everyone simultaneously. In such cases, posting important information or general handover items on the bulletin board allows Team members to check them **easily at any time**.
Example: Health checkup guide, annual leave application deadline notice, etc.

### **Posting an Article ✍️**

You can register an article by tapping **[Bulletin Board > Write Post]**.

### **Editing / Deleting an Article**

You can edit or delete an article by tapping **[Bulletin Board > Article > '⋯']**.

- **Editing**: Only the **author** can do this.

- **Deleting**: Both the **author** and the **administrator** can do this.
