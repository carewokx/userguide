## **👥 Your team: Built Together 🏢**

Now, work more efficiently with your colleagues on CareWokx by sharing work **schedules** and communicating together.

### ✨ **team Advantages**

- Manage your team just like your real-world work environment with unified **shift** types and job title displays.

- **Administrator** privileges support efficient and flexible member management.

---

## **team Creation and Joining**

### **team Creation**

- You can create a team simply by entering the team name and your name.

- Invite colleagues right away to start using it together.

### **team Joining**

- You can join instantly by entering the team's identification code, password, and the name you wish to use.

---

## **team Settings**

You can conveniently manage settings in the **[team > Tap My team Name > Gear Icon]** menu.

### **General Members**

- Change my information (**name**, **shift** end date setting)

- **Leaving the team** is possible (can leave immediately at any time)

### **Administrator**

- Change team name

- Manage member job titles

- Change my information (**name** only; cannot set **shift** end date)

- Change administrator privileges (cannot remove their own admin status)

- Change/Reset team password (must change immediately after reset)

- Modify **Shift** Types (shift display name, start-end time)

- Delete team (requires password)

### ⚠️ **Caution:** Administrators cannot set their own **shift** end date.

---

## **📅 Full Team Work Schedule**

You can intuitively check your entire team's work **schedule**.

- Easily identify colleagues working together on specific dates.

- Provides a layout similar to the actual operational work **schedule**.

- Past work information is preserved even if a member leaves.

💡 **Tip:** To view the work **schedule** before joining, the administrator can immediately grant access by changing the member's join date setting.

---

### **team Member Invitation**

The **[team Member List > Invite Button]** supports various invitation methods.

- Invite from Friend List (Valid for 24 hours)

- Invite with Identification Code (Valid for 24 hours)

- Share via Invitation Link (To be supported later)

Invite your colleagues right away.

---

### **👥 team Member Management**  
 **Administrators** can easily manage member information in the **[team > team Members]** menu.

- Member order can be changed by dragging.

- Name, job title, and **shift** end date can be easily modified.

- Members can be removed when necessary.

---

### **💬 Communication Bulletin Board**

Communicate freely on the bulletin board dedicated to your team.

- Provides functions to register, edit, and delete posts.

- Administrators can delete all posts, and authors can edit and delete their own posts.

---

### **📸 Convenient Work Schedule Photo Storage**

You can directly save paper or image-based work **schedules** to the app.

- You can quickly view them in the order they were most recently saved.

### ⚠️ **Caution:** Be aware that saved photos will be lost if the app is deleted or the device is changed.
