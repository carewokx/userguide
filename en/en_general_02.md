## Personal Data Protection

## **How CareWokx Strives to Protect Personal Data**

### **🔒 Easy and Transparent Terms of Service Confirmation**

- CareWokx immediately posts any changes to the Terms of Service on the in-app notice board and the Policy center on our website, ensuring they are transparent and accessible anytime.

- We operate a clear reporting channel at **calendar@carewokx.com** where users can report inappropriate posts or content that raises concerns about personal data infringement found on the notice board.

### **🛡️ Minimum Personal Data Collection**

- CareWokx prioritizes personal data protection above all else. We collect only the minimum amount of personal data (nickname, email, profile photo) through social login (Apple, Meta, Google) and do not collect any other unnecessary personal data.

- Personal data is immediately destroyed upon user withdrawal, ensuring enhanced security. Exceptions are made only for cases where retention is required by legal mandates.

### **📩 Fast and Accurate Complaint Handling**

- You can contact us anytime at **calendar@carewokx.com** with any inconveniences or complaints regarding personal data, and we will handle them quickly and accurately. The inquiry email address is also easily accessible within the app and on the website.

### **🗄️ Thorough Security Assurance**

- CareWokx utilizes Google Cloud Platform (GCP), which possesses a global-level security infrastructure, to safely and thoroughly protect personal data.

### **📢 Transparent Marketing and Promotion Policy**

- Marketing information is sent only to emails for which the user has given consent. We avoid contacting users via text messages or other channels, preventing unnecessary exposure of personal data.

- Even if you initially refuse to receive marketing information, you can freely change your consent status at any time via the in-app settings **[More > Service Info > Consent to Marketing and Promotion Utilization]**.

CareWokx will always prioritize the protection of your valuable personal data and strive to provide a more trustworthy service.
