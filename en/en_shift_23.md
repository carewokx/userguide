## **Annual Leave · Half-Day Leave: Easy Leave Management**

You can check your leave status at a glance, and easily manage everything from registering annual/half-day leave to tracking remaining days.

---

### **⚙️ Leave Settings**

Please enter the total number of leave days you will use this year in the **[Leave Status > Add Leave Setting]** menu.  
 The entered information is automatically reflected as follows:

- **Annual leave/Half-day leave registered on the shift schedule are automatically deducted.**

- If you have already used leave, you can enter it together to **calculate the remaining days accurately.**

- You can subsequently **modify or delete the settings** in the **[Leave Management > Edit]** menu.

---

### **🗓️ Annual/Half-Day Leave Registered on the Shift Schedule**

  When you register annual leave or half-day leave on your **shift** schedule, it is **automatically deducted** from the total number of leave days you have set.

- **Annual Leave (R)**: 1 day deducted

- **Half-Day Leave (½)**: 0.5 days deducted

- **OFF** days do not affect the number of leave days.

  ⚠️ If you do not register the leave on your **shift** schedule, it will not be deducted from your remaining leave days. Please be sure to register it.

---

### **⏰ How to Enter Half-Day Leave**

  Half-day leave can be used **together with other shift types.**  
   However, you must first have a **shift** entered for that specific date to register half-day leave.  
  For example, to enter **'DAY + Half-Day Leave' on May 21st**:

1. In **[Shift Edit]**, first **enter the DAY shift** for that date.

2. Then, select Half-Day Leave (½), and it will be registered in the **'DAY ½'** format.

   ***

### **📊 Leave Status Tab**
In the **[Leave Status]** tab, you can check your **remaining leave days based on the most recent leave setting** you entered.

If you change the leave settings, the displayed information is automatically reflected.  
  - 📌 Note that **the leave status is calculated based on all leave entered in the shift schedule, regardless of the month currently visible on the screen.**  
  For example, even if you are viewing May, the calculation of remaining days includes leave used up to July if it has been entered.
