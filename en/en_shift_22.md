## **How to Utilize Your Shift Schedule**

### **✏️ Shift Memo**

You can leave a brief memo for each **shift**.  
The content is **automatically saved when you input it and then tap another area of the screen** (without needing a separate save button).

- You can quickly write a simple memo for each **shift** without pressing a save button.

- Memos can be checked along with the **shift** type in the **[My Shift Schedule]** and **[Calendar]** tabs.

  💡 **Usage Tip**

  - If you turn on **'Notification'** in **[Shift > Shift Type Management]**, the **corresponding shift memo** is displayed along with the notification alarm. This is useful for making sure you don't forget important information!

  - Memos are not shared with Team(workplace) members; **only you** can view them.

    ***

  ### **📊 This Month's Statistics**

  You can intuitively check statistics by **shift** type in the **[My Shift Schedule > This Month's Statistics]** menu.

- Clearly shows the count for each **shift**, making it easy to understand your **shift** pattern.  
  (e.g., DAY **Shift** 5 times, NIGHT **Shift** 3 times)

  ***

  ### **📤 Shift Schedule Sharing**

- You can save your **shift** schedule as an image or easily share it with others.  
Only your **shift calendar** is shared, excluding your personal **calendar** items.

---

### 📌 How to Share

- Tap **[Shift > Share]**, and then select the month you want to share from the **shift** card: **<Previous Month / This Month / Next Month>**.

- Your name and Team(workplace) name are displayed together at the top of the shared image.

---

### ⚠️ Caution

- OFF **shifts** are not separately indicated.  
  Please note that it may be difficult to distinguish if a day is OFF or simply empty on the shared **shift** schedule.

---

### 👥 **Colleagues You Are Working With**

**👍 You can identify colleagues you will collaborate with in advance, making handovers and work coordination much easier.**

- After joining a Team(workplace), when selecting a date in **[My Shift Schedule]**, you can view the **list of colleagues you will be working with**.
- The list is organized by job title. If there are many people, you can easily view the entire list of colleagues with **[View All Members]**.

  ⚠️ If some colleagues have not entered their **shift** schedule, they may not appear in the list.

---

## **Shift Entry**

You can enter or modify your **shift calendar** in **[Shift > View Details > Edit Shift]**.

### - **Enter Day by Day:** Select the desired date, and then simply tap the corresponding **shift** in order for easy entry.

  - If you want to modify an already entered **shift**, you can simply tap the date and enter the new **shift** without needing to delete the previous one first.

### - **Pattern Repeat:** You can create a repeating rule that cycles between the start date and the end date.
