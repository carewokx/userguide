## **How are Shift Types Managed?**

The items displayed on your **shift** schedule, such as DAY, NIGHT, and OFF, are called **Shift Types**.
CareWokx allows **Shift Types** to be managed differently depending on whether they are personal or Team(workplace)-affiliated.

---

### **🧩 Default Shift Types Provided**

When you first install the app, the following **Shift Types** are provided by default.
This configuration is used commonly for both **individuals** and **Team(workplace)s**.

- **Standard Shifts**: DAY, EVE, NIGHT, MID-DAY, SPLIT, 24H
- **Custom Shifts**: C1, C2, C3 (You can freely change these names)
- **Time-Off Types**: OFF, R (Annual Leave), ½ (Half-Day Leave)

---

## **⚙️ Who Manages Shift Types?**

**Shift Types** are managed in the two ways described below:

### **1\. My Shift Types (Set by Individual)**

- A basic set is provided when you install the app.

- You can modify names, add/delete items, and set notifications.

- The **Notification feature** plays music at a set time. It only works when your device is set to Ringtone Mode.

- The **Shift Types** you modify **remain unchanged, even if you join or leave a different Team(workplace).**

### **2\. Team(workplace) Shift Types (Set by Team(workplace))**

- When you join a Team(workplace), the **Shift Types** defined by that Team(workplace) are automatically applied.

- Team(workplace) **Shift Types** can **only be modified by the administrator.**

---

## **Q. What happens to Shift Types when a Team(workplace) is deleted?**

If you tap **[My Team(workplace) Info]** at the top of the **[Shift]** tab, a list of Team(workplace)s you have belonged to is shown.
If you delete an old Team(workplace) (e.g., 'Team(workplace) 1') from this list:

- **The Shift Types used in that Team(workplace) are automatically converted to your personal Shift Types**, and
- **Your shift schedule will no longer be visible to the members remaining in that Team(workplace).**

---

## **Q. How are the Shift Start Date and Shift Schedule Viewing Range set?**

When you join a Team(workplace), your **Join Date is automatically set as the Shift Start Date.**

- If an older **shift** schedule is needed, **the administrator must adjust the Shift Start Date.**

---

## **📌 In Summary,**

- Your personal **Shift Types** and the Team(workplace) **Shift Types** are **saved separately.**

- Even if you leave or delete a Team(workplace), **the entered shift schedule does not disappear.**

- The **Shift Start Date** is automatically set, but **can be adjusted by the administrator.**
