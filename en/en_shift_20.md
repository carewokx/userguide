## Smart Personal Shift Management ⚙️

### **Shift Calendar & Detailed Info**
- Tapping [View Details] on the **shift calendar** allows you to instantly check the entered **shift** type and memo.  
  You can easily navigate between months by sliding left or right. The detailed page for a selected date displays the **Shift Type**, **Colleagues you work with**, and the **Memo** all at a glance.

- **Easy Shift Editing**

  You can directly enter or modify your **shift calendar** in **[Shift > View Details > Edit Shift]**.

  - **Enter Day by Day:** Select the desired date and tap the corresponding **shift** in order for easy entry.
  - **Pattern Repeat:** You can create a repeating rule that cycles between the start date and the end date.

- **Customize Shift Types for You**

  A variety of **shift** types are provided by default, and custom settings are also available as needed.

(When affiliated with a Team(workplace), the **shift** types of that Team(workplace) are applied.)

- **Default Shifts:** DAY, EVE, NIGHT, MID-DAY, SPLIT, 24H

  - **Customizable Shifts:** C1, C2, C3
  - **Time-Off Types:** OFF, Annual Leave (R), Half-Day Leave (½)

- You can modify the following items for each **shift** type:

  (When affiliated with a Team(workplace), only notification settings can be modified.)

- **Shift** Name Display

  - **Shift** Hours
  - Notification Settings (ON/OFF, Notification Time)

### - **Simple Sharing Function**

  With the single [Share] button, you can quickly share your **shift** schedule with friends or family. Don't worry, your personal **calendar** items are securely protected.

### - **Smart Leave Management**

  Check your leave status at a glance, and easily handle leave requests and remaining leave days.

  If you enter the total number of leave days in **[Leave Status > Add Leave Setting]**, the leave days registered on your **shift** schedule are automatically deducted. You can also enter previously used leave days for more accurate management.

  - Annual Leave (R) deducts 1 day, and Half-Day Leave (½) deducts 0.5 days.
  - OFF days do not affect the number of leave days.
  - Half-Day Leave can be registered together with other **shift** types (e.g., DAY½).
