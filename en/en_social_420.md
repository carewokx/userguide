## **👥 Groups: See Your People's Work Schedules at a Glance!**

A **Group** is a space where you can share work **schedules** and communicate with acquaintances like friends, family, or partners. Unlike a 'Workplace' used in an actual job setting, you can utilize Groups in a lighter, more flexible atmosphere.

### **✅ Group Characteristics**

- **Anyone can create and join**
   You can create a Group simply by entering the group's name, and quickly join using an **identification code**.

- **Displayed based on individual Shift Type settings**
   Even if the underlying schedule is the same, it may look different according to each person's **Shift Type** settings. This is useful for figuring out each other's **OFF** days or busy days.

- **Operated equally without an administrator**
   There's no separate administrator or title, unlike a workplace. Anyone can freely post and communicate with each other.

### **🔄 Group Operation Guide**

- **Maintenance period is 60 days**
   A Group is maintained for **60 days** from the date it was created.
   However, in **[Group Settings]**, you can **reset the maintenance period to 60 days** at any time when the remaining period is 59 days or less.
   If you use a Group frequently, you can reset it periodically to keep using it!

- **Easy to leave and delete**
   You can **leave or delete a Group** in **[Group Settings]**.
   Even if you created the Group, you are free to leave whenever you wish.
