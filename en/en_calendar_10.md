## **At-a-Glance Shift Management 🗓️**

### **"Was that day off? Can I make an appointment..?"**

Have you ever found yourself switching back and forth between your **shift** schedule and **calendar** just to make an appointment? Now, with CareWokx, you can see your work **shift calendar** and personal **calendar** all in one place.

- **See Shifts and Personal Calendars at a Glance**

  You no longer need to switch between your work **shift calendar** and personal **calendar**; with CareWokx, you can neatly view all your **calendars** on a single screen. Check important appointments and **shift calendars** simultaneously to manage your time more efficiently.

- **The Perfect Calendar Style for You**

  Choose your preferred **calendar** style: ① Monthly View, ② Monthly/Daily Combined View, or ③ Daily View. You can easily switch between views by smoothly swiping the screen up and down.

  **✨ \[Calendar] Usage Tip:**

  - For monthly navigation, **swipe left or right**!
  - You can instantly check today's **shift** and **calendar** by **double-tapping the \[Calendar] tab.**

  - You can select the **calendars** you want to use in the **[Calendar > Calendar Management]** menu. Easily manage all your schedules by linking your existing Google, Apple, and Samsung **calendars**, as well as the CareWokx internal **calendar**.

- **Daily Records, Stickers**

  Easily record your mood or special moments with stickers. You can also quickly and fun record recurring daily activities like exercise or walks with stickers. Decorate your own **calendar** vibrantly!
