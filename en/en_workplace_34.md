## **Full Team Work Schedule 🗂️ Full Work Schedule Explanation**

You can view the full work schedule in **[Team > View Full Team Work Schedule]**.  
 This screen allows you to see the work **schedules** of all current Team members at a glance.  
 Its layout is similar to a physical work **schedule**, making it convenient to check.

### **📌 Layout**

- Your **schedule** is shown at the top, and the **work schedules of all members** are shown below.
- **Job title and name** are displayed on the left.
- **Detailed information for the selected date** is also displayed at the bottom of the work **schedule**.

---

## **📅 How to Navigate the Work Schedule**

- **Scroll left and right** to move between dates

- **Select Year/Month** to view different months
   ※ Available to view starting from the month you joined the Team

- **Pull down the bottom bar** to view in full-screen mode

- **Scroll up and down** to view when there are many members
   → Your **schedule** is always fixed at the top.

---

## **👥 Details Page**

Information for the selected date is displayed at the bottom of the work **schedule**.

- The specific date

- Colleagues working together

  - Number of people

  - List by job title

  - Available to view the full list with **[View All Members]**

👍 This is useful for **shift** handovers and collaboration, as you can see the people working together at a glance!

---

### **ℹ️ Please Note**

- The member order within the work **schedule** can **only be changed by the administrator** in **[Team > Team Members]**.
  Since changing the member order affects the entire work **schedule** layout, administrators should set it carefully to match the team's composition.
