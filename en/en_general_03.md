## **'More' Tab Guide**

In the 'More' tab, you can easily view and edit your information.
It also provides Customer Service features to help you use CareWokx more conveniently and securely.

---

### **👤 User Information**

**[More > 'My Profile' at the Top]**

- You can see your profile photo and name at a glance.

- The profile information from the SNS account linked during sign-up is automatically applied. You can change your name freely at any time.

- You cannot use vulgar or inappropriate expressions in your name.

- You can check your personal identification code.
   👉 Tapping the code automatically copies it, making it easy to use when adding friends or inviting people to a group.

- You can check your currently linked SNS accounts and easily add others by tapping the [Link] button.

---

### **🛎️ Customer Service**

**[More > Available under each section]**

- **Notices**
   Quickly check CareWokx updates and important announcements.

- **Service Info**

- **Privacy Policy / Terms of Service / Operating Policy**
   You can tap each item to view detailed contents anytime.
   ✔️ We clearly guide you through the content to protect user rights and ensure transparent operation.

- **Push Notification Settings**
  Set this to ON to receive notifications from CareWokx.

- **Consent to Marketing and Promotion Utilization**
   You can change your consent status at any time. If you consent, useful updates or news will be sent to your email. **Don't worry, we won't contact you via text message or phone call.**

---

### **🔒 Log Out & Account Deactivation**

- **Log Out**
   You can log out to switch accounts or for security purposes.

- **Account Deactivation**
   All personal data is immediately deleted upon deactivation.
   ✔️ Your personal data is thoroughly protected. Please decide carefully, as recovery is impossible.

---

### **❓ FAQ & Contact Us**

- **Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)**
   Check common questions and their answers.

- **Contact Us**
   You can send an email to **calendar@carewokx.com**.
   Tapping the button automatically connects you to the mail app installed on your device.

  Feel free to send us any questions or requests you have at any time. We will review them as quickly as possible and provide a sincere response.
