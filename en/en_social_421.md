## **Group Creation/Invitation/Joining/Leaving & Bulletin Board/Work Schedule**

### **🔹 Group Creation**

You can create a group instantly by just entering the group name in **[Social > Create Group]**.
Start easily with just a simple input!

---

### **🔹 Joining a Group**

- You can **join up to 20 groups**.

- **You can join immediately by entering only the identification code.**

💡 _The Group Identification Code is automatically issued when the group is created._

---

### **🔹 How to Invite to a Group**

[Social > Group > Member Count > Add Friend Button]

**👥 Invite from Friend List (Valid for 24 hours)**

- Only friends who can be invited are displayed in the list.
  **🔑 Invite with Personal Identification Code (Valid for 24 hours)**
- Even if they are not friends, you can **invite someone by directly entering their personal identification code.**
- You can check your personal identification code in the [More] tab, beneath your name.
  **🔗 Share Invitation Link**
- You can invite friends by sharing the generated link via messenger or social media.

---

### **🔹 Leaving a Group**

You can **leave the group at any time** via the path **[Social > Group > Gear Icon > Leave Group]**.

---

### **🔹 Kicking Out a Group Member**

By tapping **[Social > Group > Member Count > Kick Out]**, you can **remove a group member.**
*Note: **Anyone has the authority to kick out members**, so caution is required when operating the group.*

📌 The kicked-out member does not receive a separate notification, but the group will **automatically disappear from their Group List.**

---

## **📆 Checking the Group Work Schedule**

You can **check the work schedules** of members affiliated with the group in **[Social > Group]**. **In most respects, it is the same as the full work schedule of a workplace.**

- The group work schedule screen is similar to the workplace work schedule, but the **displayed shift types are based on each member's personal settings.**
   → Therefore, the same work schedule may look different to each member.

---

## **📢 Group Bulletin Board**

Feel free to share important news or stories you want to share with your group members!
You can **register, modify, and delete posts**, just like on the workplace bulletin board.

⚠️ Caution

- **Anyone can delete a post.**

- **The author's name is not displayed on the post.**
   Therefore, please be mindful when creating content.
