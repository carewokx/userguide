## **🔔 Alert (Notification) Settings Guide**

If you set up a notification (alert) for your **shift** or **calendar** item, you can receive an alert at the designated time. Set up notifications in advance so you don't miss any important **shifts** or appointments!

## ⚠️ **Only works when Device Notifications (Push Notifications) are ON.**

### **📌 Shift Notifications**

**Shift** notifications can be set in **[Shift > Shift Type Management > Each Shift Type]**.

- After turning on the notification toggle and setting the time, the **shift type and shift memo** will be displayed on the screen **along with music** at the specified time.

- You can set alerts at different times for each **shift** type. Utilize this flexibly according to your own **calendar**!

### **⚠️ Caution**

- Music alerts only play when the device is in **Ringtone Mode**. The music cannot be changed.

- If you change your Team(workplace) ↔ personal affiliation, **all notifications will be reset to OFF.** Be sure to check your notification settings again after changing your affiliation.

---

### **📌 Calendar Item Notifications**

**Calendar** item notifications can be set on the **Calendar Item Add/Edit screen**.

- You can receive an alert at your desired time based on the 'Start Time' of the set **calendar** item, such as **on the hour, 10 minutes before, or 1 hour before**.

- **Calendar** item notifications **only provide text alerts and sound effects**; music will not be played.
