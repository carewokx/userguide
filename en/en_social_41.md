## **Add/Delete Friends**

Anyone added as a 'Friend' can be invited to your workplace or group at any time.
Furthermore, you can check your friend's schedule in the group **shift** schedule, making it convenient when coordinating plans together.

### **✅ Add Friend**

[Social > Friend List > Add Friend Button]

- You can **send a friend request by entering your friend's identification code.**

- Alternatively, you can **send a friend request by sharing an invitation link.**

- The person who receives the friend request **must accept it to be registered as a friend.**

**⚠️ The request must be accepted within 24 hours of being sent**; it will expire after this time. **If it expires, simply send the request again.**

### **❌ Delete Friend**

- The delete button appears when you **swipe left** on the friend in the friend list.

- Deleting a friend **does not affect any workplaces or groups they are already participating in.**
